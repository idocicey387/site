Product Portfolio

Three products,
one mission

We're building a suite of mobile-first sports applications that cover the full spectrum of the modern fan experience — from news consumption to deep analytics and community.

Media Aggregation

Fieldstream

Fieldstream is our flagship sports news aggregation platform — a single, beautifully designed destination for fans who want the full picture without the noise. The app pulls content from hundreds of vetted sources across 40+ sports and 60+ leagues, filtered intelligently by the teams, athletes and competitions each user actually cares about.

Unlike generic news apps, Fieldstream understands sports context. It knows the difference between a transfer rumour and confirmed news, surfaces match previews at exactly the right time, and delivers post-match analysis the moment the final whistle blows.

  • → Personalised multi-sport content feeds based on preference profiles
  • → Real-time push notifications for breaking news and match events
  • → AI-powered content ranking that learns from your reading habits
  • → Offline reading mode for commutes and low-connectivity environments
  • → Available on Android and iOS, with tablet-optimised layouts
Fieldstream — Media App
Live Analytics

StatEdge

StatEdge is a professional-grade sports analytics application designed for the fan who wants to go deeper than the scoreboard. Powered by real-time data feeds from across the globe, it delivers the kind of statistical depth previously reserved for club analysts and broadcast professionals — presented in a format accessible to any fan.

From expected goals and passing networks to historical head-to-head comparisons and player performance curves, StatEdge transforms raw sports data into stories that make the game richer and more engaging.

  • → Live match data with sub-second event latency
  • → Advanced visualisations: heat maps, passing networks, shot charts
  • → Historical database covering 10+ years of competition data
  • → Multi-sport coverage: football, basketball, tennis, and more
  • → Customisable widgets and comparison tools
StatEdge — Analytics App
Fan Community

TerraceTalk

TerraceTalk is a fan-community platform that brings the energy of the stadium terrace to the digital world. Built around club-specific communities, it gives supporters a dedicated space to share reactions, debate tactics, celebrate wins and commiserate defeats — without the noise of general social media.

The platform integrates live match contexts so that community activity spikes at the moments that matter: lineups announced, goals scored, final whistles blown. Fan engagement metrics are our north star, and every feature we build serves the goal of deepening the connection between supporters and their clubs.

  • → Club-specific community spaces with verified supporter groups
  • → Match threads that sync live with real-time event data
  • → Prediction leagues and fan polls tied to upcoming fixtures
  • → Fan achievement badges, season streaks and leaderboards
  • → Moderated environments with robust community guidelines
TerraceTalk — Fan Platform
