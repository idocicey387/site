Our Story

Built by fans,
driven by data

We started as a small internal team inside a larger European group. Today, Totalbet Digital is a focused, independent media technology studio.

The Origin

From holding group to independent studio

Totalbet Digital was spun out in 2024 from the broader TOTALBET Zakłady Bukmacherskie group. As digital consumption of sports content exploded globally, leadership recognised that serving modern fans required a dedicated team with a singular focus: technology and media.

Rather than treating digital products as a side initiative, the decision was made to form a separate entity with its own engineering culture, product roadmap and creative direction. That entity is Totalbet Digital.

We operate from Warsaw, Poland, with engineers and designers working across European time zones. Our parent group's deep domain knowledge in sports gives us a unique advantage: we understand what fans actually care about.

Est. 2024, Warsaw
Our Mission

"Deliver emotion
through digital experience"

Sports are fundamentally emotional. A last-minute goal, a record broken, a comeback nobody expected — these moments define why fans keep coming back. Our products exist to amplify those emotions, to put the right information in the right hands at exactly the right moment, and to connect fans with the stories that matter to them most.

How We Work

Principles that guide us

Mobile-First, Always

Sports moments don't happen at desks. Every product we build is designed for the screen in your hand, with performance and usability that works in a packed stadium just as well as on a commute.

Data Without Complexity

We believe professional-grade analytics should not require a degree to understand. Our UX philosophy strips away complexity so that depth is always accessible, never intimidating.

Fan-Centric Design

We are sports fans ourselves. Every feature we ship gets tested against one question: "Would I actually use this?" If the answer isn't an enthusiastic yes, it doesn't ship.

Privacy by Design

Trust is the foundation of every user relationship. We collect only what we need, protect what we hold, and are transparent about how data flows through our products.

A team that's growing fast

We're a diverse, talented group of engineers, designers, and product thinkers. We're actively hiring across roles — if you're passionate about sports and technology, we'd love to talk.
