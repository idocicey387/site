Latest Announcements

2026 Highlights

March 2026

Totalbet Digital Announces Public Beta of Fieldstream for Android

After six months of closed alpha testing with an invited community of 2,000 sports fans across Poland and the UK, our flagship media aggregation app Fieldstream is entering a public beta phase on Android. The beta includes personalised feeds for football, basketball and tennis, covering over 40 major competitions worldwide.

We are inviting sports fans to join the beta programme via the Google Play Early Access page and to share feedback that will shape the final v1.0 release, planned for Q2 2026.

February 2026

StatEdge Secures Data Partnership with Leading European Sports Data Provider

Totalbet Digital has finalised a multi-year data licensing agreement with a leading European sports data aggregator, unlocking access to live event feeds, historical datasets and advanced metrics for over 800 competitions across 35 sports.

This partnership forms the core data layer for StatEdge and elevates the depth and reliability of the analytics available to fans inside the application. Integration work is underway and the first data-powered features are expected in Q2 2026.

January 2026

Totalbet Digital Launches TerraceTalk Pilot with Four Football Club Communities

Our fan community platform TerraceTalk has launched a structured pilot programme in collaboration with the supporter associations of four professional football clubs in Poland and Central Europe. The pilot, running through Q2 2026, will test the platform's core community and match-thread features with real fan cohorts.

Learnings from the pilot will directly inform the product's roadmap and feature prioritisation ahead of a broader rollout planned for the second half of 2026.

TOTALBET Zakłady Bukmacherskie Sp. z o.o. announces the formal establishment of Totalbet Digital as its dedicated media technology division, with a mandate to build and grow a portfolio of sports media mobile applications. Looking Ahead

2026 Roadmap Highlights

Q2 2026 — Fieldstream v1.0 Launch +

Following the successful public beta, Fieldstream v1.0 will launch on both Google Play and the App Store. The full release includes a redesigned onboarding experience, expanded content partner network (50+ sources), enhanced push notification controls, and the first version of our AI-powered content ranking engine.

Q2 2026 — StatEdge Early Access +

StatEdge enters Early Access on Android with live data for the top 5 European football leagues, the NBA, and the ATP/WTA tennis tours. Key Q2 features include live heat maps, player comparison tools, and a customisable statistics dashboard. iOS version follows in Q3.

Q3 2026 — TerraceTalk General Availability +

Based on pilot learnings, TerraceTalk will open to any fan community globally in Q3. The GA release introduces prediction leagues, supporter group verification, and a revamped match thread experience. We plan to support 200+ clubs at launch across European football's top 10 leagues.

Q4 2026 — Platform Integration & Cross-Product Features +

In Q4 we begin the work of connecting our three products into a coherent platform experience. Users will be able to move seamlessly from a Fieldstream news article to the relevant StatEdge match page, or share a statistical insight directly into a TerraceTalk community. A unified account system and cross-product notifications will ship in this phase.
